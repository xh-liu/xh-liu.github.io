About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Multimedia Lab (MMLab)(established by Prof. Xiaoou Tang), the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang and Prof. Hongsheng Li. Before that, I received bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering in Tsinghua University (THU) in 2017. My research interests are computer vision and deep learning.
